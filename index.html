East London Mystery Club

Unleash your inner Sherlock Holmes and solve real-life mysteries

Come join the East London Mystery Club and challenge yourself with our tailored events.
Gather your friends, put on your detective hats and crack the case!

We are a London-based club whose focus is to offer entertaining and enjoyable events that will make you feel like an actual detective trying to solve a mystery. Our events are a low-cost, but high quality pastime and we organize events suited for beginners and experienced detectives alike.

The events take place once in every two weeks and the registration opens a month before the event. Due to the popularity of these events, they tend to fill up quickly, so make your reservations well ahead of time!

The events are open to everybody, but if you join our membership program, you'll gain access to exclusive events and discounts — register as a member today, it's free!
